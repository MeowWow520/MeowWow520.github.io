A succubus (pl. succubi) is a female demon who was described in various European folklore as appearing in the dreams of male humans in order to seduce them. Repeated interactions between a succubus and a man will lead to sexual activity. The establishment and perpetuation of such a relationship enables the production of a hybrid child known as a cambion, but at the expense of the man, whose mental and physical health will deteriorate rapidly, eventually resulting in his death if the succubus continues courting him for a protracted period.

The following description of “succubus” isn’t tranditionally supernatural monster, but an ideation adout the extreme love for beer even to the extent that it causes disordered will and wanna to “eat” someone.

「Primitive societies believed that alcoholic beverages possessed supernatural properties. Beer could induce a blurred state of consciousness and a trance-like, euphoric sensation.」”The blurred state of consciousness“ is helpful for motivating the subconscious in the depth of people’s inner hearts. “The trance-like, euphoric sensation“ also contributes the eruption of imagination. Botan Kamiina(also called Botan or Kamiina), who is a collage freshman, originally is banned from drinking beverages containing alcohol compounds, but by reasonable loopholes, this anime let Botan forcibly improve 1yo. this brings a strange sense of disobedience in public trendency. In other freshman, this strange sense of disobedience seems unconventional and unusual. Tiny difference on the age is to open up and become an huge ravines. this ravines between general students and repeat students, for one this gives a feeling that visible but unreachable, for another it also enhanced sense of mystery for Botan. At the definition of succubus in this article, this kind of age status posture as if she’s the only one that matters undoubtedly can foreshadow the image of Botan Kamiina‘s succubus.

Furthermore, language expression ability can also highlight the image of Botan Kamiina‘s succubus. it is divided into two periods when Botan Kamiina express her feelings. one is “the obedient girl period” without alcohol, in this period, Botan Kamiina acted as a obedient cat, no horrible words, and filled with gentleness and kindness.
